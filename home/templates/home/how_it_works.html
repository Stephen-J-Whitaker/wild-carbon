More about our service

In terms of offsetting your life's estimated carbon footprint, commissioning the planting of around 1000 trees is a good target (see our facts section below for more information). However, any trees that are planted will not only sequester carbon offsetting your carbon footprint but will also help to 'rewild' land that would otherwise be left in a cultivated state.

What is 'rewilding', you might ask. Well, rewilding is the process of restoring an area of land to its natural state by reintroducing species of flora and fauna that have been driven out or removed.

Your purchase of our carbon sequestration through rewilding service directly contributes to the reintroduction of native tree species that were removed from the site. Alongside the planting of trees, we do all we can to help improve the biodiversity of the site and protect it forever.

Profits from our service contribute to the ongoing protection of our currently owned location in the west of Ireland and, very soon, the purchase of additional suitable locations around the country of Ireland.

To ensure you feel safe putting your trust in us, we are externally audited and log the planting of every tree planted at our location. On planting of your commissioned trees, you will receive a digital certificate detailing the trees planted on your behalf, via email.

Wild Facts

  • The estimated carbon footprint of a person in Ireland is currently approximately 13 tons of CO2 per year

  • A mature tree will sequester approximately 20kg of CO2 per year but an immature tree might only absorb 10kg of carbon per year

    The amount of carbon absorbed also varies with the species and location of the tree, amongst many other things. For this reason we estimate the absorption of carbon dioxide to be 10 kg per year. It is a conservative estimate

  • The average life expectancy of a person in Ireland is 80 years
  • The trees will be allowed to grow for their entire natural life but we use a tree life expectancy of 100 years for our estimates.
  • The average person needs to plant approximately ((13000 x 80) / 10) / 100 = 1040 trees
  • Approximately 640 trees per acre.
  • Approximately 640 x 80 = 51200 trees can be supported by our location
  • It can take up to 2 years from the order being placed to the tree being planted out depending on the time of year you placed your order. The trees seeds need germinating (we grow from seed to ensure genetic diversity in our stock), growing on and then planting out when they are of the correct size for the tree in question.
